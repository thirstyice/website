Rules of the Northern Mountainous Republic

  1. No building anything unnatural-looking that is visible from the outside
    Save for things meant to be seen by the public, keep them small
    Maximum sentence: Fixing the problem

  2. No claiming more mountains until there is no room left in your first mountain
    1. Amount of room must be verified by another person
    2. Verification must be posted in the lower right of the rules wall
    3. Maximum ownership of 2 mountains
    Maximum sentence: Filling the mountain back in with appropriate materials
